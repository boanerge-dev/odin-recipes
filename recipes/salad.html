Description

A fresh salad with crisp vegetables, a light dressing, and optional toppings like cheese or nuts.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop vegetables.
  2. Mix in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss and serve fresh.
