Description

This pizza is made with a crispy crust, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni (optional)
  • Vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake until golden brown and bubbly.
